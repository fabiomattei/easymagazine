#INSTALLATION#
1. Choose your language:
-Skinbu-EN for English language
-Skinbu-IT for Italian language
2. Upload the folder with the language you want in [WORDPRESS DIRECTORY]/wp-content/themes
3. Go to your wordpress admin cp>theme>active Skinbu Theme

Go to http://www.skimbu.it/projects/temi-wordpress/skinbu/ to know more about this theme!
